AALAS Foundation Appreciation Reception & Live Auction

When: Wednesday, November 12th
6:30 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.

Where: Hyatt Hotel, Regency Ballroom B


Join the AALAS Foundation as we celebrate the benefactors and volunteers who generously support the AALAS Foundation.

Dress up in your favorite casual beachwear attire - California style!

Enjoy food and a cash bar while bidding on outstanding auction items generously donated and up for bid this year.

All proceeds to benefit the AALAS Foundation.
