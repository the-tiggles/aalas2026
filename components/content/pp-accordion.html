What is the AALAS Foundation?

The AALAS Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that promotes public understanding of responsible laboratory animal care and use in scientific research. We provide free educational materials, outreach resources, and programs that highlight how research with animals contributes to improving human and animal health.

How is the AALAS Foundation related to AALAS?

The AALAS Foundation supports the mission of the American Association for Laboratory Animal Science (AALAS) by focusing on public awareness and education. While AALAS serves members of the laboratory animal science profession, the Foundation engages the public to build understanding and support for biomedical research.
